About Me

Committed to guiding individuals toward sustainable health. My practice is rooted in identifying and addressing the root causes of illness rather than masking symptoms.

Dis'ease' is a state of not being at 'ease'.To shift to a state of ease, we must recognize the body’s imbalance rather than suppress the signals. As a naturopath, I practice the principle of doctor as teacher guiding individuals to understand their bodies, make mindful choices, and sustain their own wellbeing.

For me, sustainable health is not about restrictive diets or cheat meals. It is about moderation, balance, and conscious living that makes you happily embrace food, lifestyle, and habits for long-term wellbeing.

TREATMENTS

Treatments are rooted in natural, non-invasive healing and aim to restore balance to the body and mind

Women's Health

Fertility issues such as PCOS, fibroids, and hormonal imbalances often arise when the body’s natural rhythm is disturbed.

My approach focuses on restoring balance through mindful nutrition, lifestyle adjustments, and natural therapies that work with the body’s cycles.

Preparing for pregnancy is not just physical, it is about nurturing a new vital force into the universe. A mother’s wellbeing shapes the child’s health and future, making it essential to prepare mentally, physically, and spiritually.

In today’s world of information overload and endless suggestions, I help women find clarity and confidence to embrace motherhood the natural, balanced way before, during, and after pregnancy.

Gut Health

The gut is often called the “second brain” because of its powerful influence on both body and mind. In reality, you are not one but TWO the human cells and the trillions of gut microbes that live within you. These microbiomes control digestion, nutrient absorption, immunity, and even mood. When the gut is out of balance, it not only causes acidity, bloating, or constipation, but can also trigger autoimmune reactions and chronic inflammation.

My approach is to restore gut harmony by rebuilding the microbiome, strengthening digestion, and correcting lifestyle habits that damage it. By doing so, we support better nutrient absorption, calm inflammation, and strengthen immunity thereby, laying the foundation for long-term healing.

Mental Health

Mental health has become one of the biggest concerns of our generation. Toxic work cultures, constant stress, and fast lifestyles have made anxiety, depression, and even dementia common words, something unheard of just 15 years ago. Drugs or intoxication may numb the pain temporarily, but they cannot be the solution.

True healing requires guided therapies with better clarity. Here, I help individuals through gold-standard yogic philosophies that harmonise body, mind, and soul. As Patanjali said, “Yogaś citta-vṛtti-nirodhaḥ"- Yoga is the cessation of the fluctuations of the mind

Sleep Issues

Who can live without sleep? It is one of the basic essentials of human life, and without it, every aspect of health suffers. Lack of sleep brings pain, poor mind–body coordination, weak immunity, and even damage to skin and hair. Sleep is the most fundamental step for upgraded wellness.

My approach is to first identify the root cause of insomnia or disturbed sleep and then restore balance through breathing techniques, lifestyle changes that restore circardian rhythm and modern techniques like sound therapy and guided relaxation that naturally regulate sleep. Deep, natural sleep transforms health at every level. With the right guidance, nights become restorative, and days begin with clarity and strength.

Strengthening immune system

The immune system gives strong signals whenever a foreign body enters, it may show up as fever, cold, cough, skin eruptions, or inflammation. These acute reactions are not problems in themselves; they are the body’s intelligent way of eliminating toxins and restoring balance. This phenomenon of eliminating toxins is called Healing crisis . But when we constantly suppress these natural responses with quick fixes, toxins keep piling up, and over time this burden reflects as major chronic diseases. This phenomenon of suppressing the symptoms is called Disease crisis

My approach is to support the immune system so that it can pass through the healing crisis safely and naturally, restoring strength. This way, we make immunity not just a defense, but the foundation of lasting wellness.

Energy Restoration

Many people today feel tired all day, even after 7–8 hours of sleep. This constant fatigue leads to procrastination, reduced productivity, and eventually mental stress. Often, we blame ourselves harshly thinking it’s laziness or lack of discipline without realising the deeper cause.

The truth is, it’s not you. It is often the error of your mitochondria and gut flora. Mitochondria, the energy factories of your cells, when weakened by poor nutrition, toxins, or stress, fail to generate enough energy. Similarly, imbalances in gut microbes reduce nutrient absorption, leaving the body starved of fuel despite eating.

Here, I focus on restoring cellular energy by strengthening mitochondria and rebuilding gut flora with the modern detox methods, right nourishment and balancing lifestyle habits.

Diabetes

Type 2 diabetes, hypertension, and high cholesterol are interconnected metabolic disorders that arise when the body’s natural balance is disturbed. Excess visceral fat, combined with poor lifestyle habits like sedentary habits, poor food choices, stress, and even gut imbalances all contribute to the problem. Unless these lifestyle patterns are addressed, no pill can create lasting change.

My approach is to conduct a detailed analysis of each individual’s health, identify the root cause, and design a treatment plan that restores balance through food, movement, and natural therapies. The goal is not just controlling numbers, but regaining long-term metabolic health.

Thyroid

Thyroid disorders whether hypothyroidism, hyperthyroidism, or autoimmune conditions like Hashimoto’s are increasingly common, especially among women. The thyroid gland may be small, but it controls metabolism, energy, mood, and even fertility. When it is out of balance, the entire body feels the impact.

Many people are unaware whether their condition is functional or autoimmune thyroidism yet, the treatment protocols for each are completely different. Following the same routine for every thyroid case will never bring lasting results.

My approach is to identify the type and cause of imbalance through detailed analysis, and then restore thyroid health using nutrition, detoxification, stress management, and supportive therapies. This way, energy, metabolism, and overall wellbeing can be truly regained.

Weight Loss program

Weight loss today has become a billion-dollar business. Quick fixes and crash diets often do more harm than good. Most often these methods are temporary, leaving people frustrated and disappointed. My approach is different. I do not sell a “one-size-fits-all” programme. True weight management is not about starving the body or chasing numbers on a scale but it is about restoring balance. The result is not just fat loss, but renewed energy, confidence, and long-term wellbeing.

Paediatric Nutrition (from weaning period)

The foundation of lifelong health is built in childhood. Good nutrition during the early years supports growth, immunity, brain development, and emotional wellbeing. Yet, many children today face challenges such as picky eating, obesity, digestive issues, allergies, or frequent infections all signs that their nutritional needs are not being met in the right way.

In my approach, pediatric nutrition is not about forcing diets or following internet trends, but about creating balanced, wholesome food habits that a child can enjoy and sustain.I help parents with easy tips and tricks to handle tantrums and gently build smooth eating habits in their children. When children learn to eat consciously and joyfully, they carry that strength into adulthood.

Detox

Detoxification is one of the most essential steps in nature cure because toxins are at the root of many illnesses. The body naturally eliminates waste through organs like the liver, kidneys, skin, lungs, and intestines but poor lifestyle, processed food, pollution, and stress overload these systems. When elimination slows down, toxins accumulate, leading to fatigue, skin issues, digestive problems, hormonal imbalances, and eventually chronic diseases.

Detox is not about crash diets, extreme fasting, or quick-fix juice cleanses. It is about supporting the body’s natural elimination channels through balanced food, hydrotherapy, yoga, fasting therapies, and lifestyle corrections. Detox is not just cleansing but an experience to see new version of yourself

Acupuncture

Acupuncture is a time-tested therapy from Chinese medicine that works on the body’s energy channels. It is an intricate system that is nothing short of magical. These channels are closely related to the varma points described in Indian healing traditions. When stimulated with precision, they restore balance, relieve pain, and ease blockages in the flow of energy.

Male Fertility Issues

A healthy baby begins with the health of both parents. While much focus is placed on the mother, male fertility plays an equally vital role in conception and in ensuring an uncomplicated pregnancy. Many men are unaware that their lifestyle, nutrition, stress, and overall health directly influence sperm quality. Lower libido has become increasingly common among today’s generation, affecting not just intimacy but also overall wellbeing.

Through naturopathic care, I work on improving male reproductive health by addressing the root causes balancing hormones, reducing toxins, managing stress, and strengthening vitality. By restoring overall health, men can play their true part in bringing a strong new life into the world.
